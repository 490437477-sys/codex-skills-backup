# noon Ramadan & MENA Seasonal Calendar

Used by: noon-niche-finder, noon-advertising-strategy, noon-sales-estimator, noon-profit-analyzer.

This calendar maps Hijri dates to Gregorian dates for 2026-2030 so you can plan inventory, ad budget, and category selection around MENA retail peaks. Dates below are estimates; confirm against the official Hijri calendar each year.

## Annual retail peaks

| Event | Approx Gregorian | Demand multiplier | Top categories |
|-------|------------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Ramadan | Feb-May (varies) | 1.5x - 4x | Food, gifting, home, family, modest fashion |
| Eid al-Fitr | After Ramadan | 1.5x - 2x | Apparel, gifts, decor, sweets |
| White Friday | Last week Nov | 3x - 5x | Everything (noon flagship event) |
| Back-to-school KSA/UAE | Aug-Sep | 1.3x - 1.8x | Stationery, electronics, kids |
| National Day KSA | 23 Sep | 1.2x - 1.5x | Saudi-themed, green/white, patriotic |
| National Day UAE | 2 Dec | 1.2x - 1.5x | UAE-themed, red/green/black/white |
| Eid al-Adha | May-Jun | 1.3x - 1.7x | Apparel, gifts, perfumery |
| Ramadan prep | 30 days before | inventory must arrive | All FBN SKUs ramp up |
| Hajj season | 60 days before Hajj | 1.4x - 1.8x | Travel gear, prayer items, modest apparel |

## Lead-time guidance

- **Inventory for Ramadan must land at FBN 45-60 days before 1 Ramadan** (not before; storage fees will eat margin).
- **White Friday inventory must land at FBN by early November.**
- **Back-to-school inventory: mid-July for KSA, early August for UAE.**
- **Ramadan digital marketing ramp-up: 30 days before.**

## Category-specific Ramadan winners

- **Food & beverage**: dates, juices, frozen samboosa, packaged sweets, Arabic coffee
- **Home & kitchen**: lanterns (fanous), decorative plates, prayer rugs, dates containers
- **Modest fashion**: abayas, kaftans, modest dresses, jalabiyya
- **Gifting**: oud, perfumes, prayer beads, kids toys, gold-plated accessories
- **Tech**: tablets for kids, smartphones (new model launches in March), smartwatches
- **Family**: board games, family activity kits, kids educational toys

- **noon-sales-estimator**: annual sales = baseline x sum of (monthly multiplier)
