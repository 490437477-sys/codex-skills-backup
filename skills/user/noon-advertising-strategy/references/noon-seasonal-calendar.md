# noon Annual Seasonal Calendar for Advertising

Used by: noon-advertising-strategy, noon-niche-finder, noon-sales-estimator.

A 12-month planning view of MENA retail peaks with budget, bid, and creative guidance per peak. Pair with `noon-ramadan-calendar.md` for Hijri date conversion.

## Annual overview

| Month | Major event | Demand lift | Bid multiplier | Inventory lead-time |
|-------|-------------|-------------|----------------|---------------------|
| January | New Year recovery | 0.9x | 1.0x | restock early Feb |
| February | Ramadan start (varies) | 1.5-4x | 1.3x (4 weeks before) | LAND 45-60 days before |
| March | Ramadan peak / Eid al-Fitr | 2-4x | 1.5x peak week | keep stock high |
| April | Eid al-Fitr / back to school prep | 1.5-2x | 1.2x | restock for back-to-school |
| May | Hajj / Eid al-Adha | 1.4-1.8x | 1.3x | restock modest apparel, gifts |
| June | Summer slow | 0.9x | 0.9x | opportunity for clearance |
| July | Summer prep | 1.0x | 1.0x | restock back-to-school |
| August | Back-to-school KSA/UAE | 1.3-1.8x | 1.3x | LAND by mid-July |
| September | National Day KSA (23 Sep) | 1.2-1.5x | 1.2x | patriotic SKUs ready |
| October | Pre-White Friday | 1.2x | 1.1x | LAND White Friday stock |
| November | White Friday week | 3-5x | 1.8x peak week | biggest push of the year |
| December | Recovery / Year-end | 1.1x | 1.0x | restock for Q1 |

## Ramadan playbook (4 weeks)

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| 4 weeks before | Bid +15% on broad/auto. Launch Ramadan-themed Sponsored Brands. |
| 3 weeks before | Bid +25%. Push gifting, food, home, modest fashion creatives. |
| 2 weeks before | Bid +30%. Enable dayparting for iftar (sunset) and suhoor (pre-dawn). |
| 1 week before | Bid +40%. Max inventory. Strongest dayparting. |
| Ramadan week 1 | Bid +30%. Volume peaks. |
| Ramadan week 2-3 | Plateau. Maintain bids. Push family entertainment SKUs. |
| Ramadan week 4 | Decline. Bid +20% for Laylat al-Qadr spike. |
| Eid al-Fitr | Bid +50% for 3 days. Apparel, gifts, decor. |

## White Friday playbook

| Phase | Timing | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Pre-tease | Early November | Brand awareness campaigns, sneak peeks |
| Pre-launch | 7 days before | Email capture, wishlist, retargeting |
| Launch day | Last Friday of November | Bid +100%, full funnel push, all channels |
| Cyber Week | Following Mon-Wed | Maintain bid, focus on conversion |
| Recovery | Following week | Lower bid, harvest remarketing audiences |

## Back-to-school playbook (Aug-Sep)

| Category | Pre-stock by | Bid start | Peak |
|----------|--------------|-----------|------|
| Stationery | Mid-July | Early Aug | Late Aug |
| Laptops / tablets | Early August | Mid-Aug | First week of school |
| School bags | Mid-July | Early Aug | Late Aug |
| Kids uniforms | Mid-July | Early Aug | Late Aug |

## National Day playbook (KSA 23 Sep, UAE 2 Dec)

- Launch patriotic themed SKUs 2 weeks before
- Use green/white (KSA) or red/green/black/white (UAE) color schemes in creatives
- Bid +20% on national-day themed keywords
- Stop the campaign 1 week after to avoid post-peak waste

## Bid dayparting (MENA timezones)

| Block | Time (AST / GST) | Conversion quality |
|-------|------------------|--------------------|
| Late night | 00:00-06:00 | Low (mostly browsing) |
| Morning | 06:00-12:00 | Medium (work hours) |
| Afternoon | 12:00-17:00 | High (lunch browsing) |
| Evening | 17:00-22:00 | Highest (post-work shopping) |
| Late evening | 22:00-24:00 | Medium-high |

For Ramadan, add iftar spike (15-30 min before sunset, exact time shifts daily) and suhoor spike (2 hours before sunrise).

## Budget allocation by quarter

| Quarter | Suggested split | Reason |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Q1 | 25% | Ramadan + Eid al-Fitr spike |
| Q2 | 20% | Hajj / Eid al-Adha |
| Q3 | 25% | Back-to-school + National Day |
| Q4 | 30% | White Friday is the biggest event |
