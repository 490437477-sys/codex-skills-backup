# Mercado Libre Rich Content 🌎🌎

Build high-converting Mercado Libre product pages with structured descripcion, complete ficha tecnica, optimized foto and video sequencing, and seasonal banners for LATAM mega-events.

## When to Use

Activate this skill whenever the user mentions:
- 美客多富文本, 美客多详情页, 美客多详情优化, 商品详情优化
- Mercado Libre rich content, 美客多图文优化
- Descripcion larga mercado libre, ficha tecnica completa
- Rich content MLM, conteudo rico MLB
- Mercado Libre product photos, MercadoLibre HD video
- Improve conversion rate on Mercado Libre listing
- Hot Sale / Buen Fin / Black Friday rich content module
- Mercado Envios FREE badge eligibility, Full optimization

## Capabilities

- **Descripcion larga structure**: 5000-char descripcion with hero hook, 3-5 beneficio bullets, especificaciones tab, uso recomendado, garantia, preguntas frecuentes, and CTA
- **Ficha tecnica completeness**: Per-category attribute map for electronics, fashion, home, beauty, baby, sports. Fill 100 percent of required and 80 percent of optional ficha tecnica lines
- **Foto sequencing**: 10-foto storyboard: hero on white, lifestyle 1, lifestyle 2, scale / size context, detail close-up, comparison vs competitor, in-use, packaging, certifications, garantia badge
- **Video script**: 30-60 second HD video with hook in first 3 seconds, demo, 3 beneficio voiceover lines, oferta call-out, CTA. Localize voiceover in Spanish (MX/AR/CL/CO) or Portuguese (BR)
- **Pack / multi-SKU presentations**: For listings with variantes build the pack module showing the full SKU range with pricing ladder, cuota breakdown, and Mercado Envios availability per variant
- **Mercado Pago cuota call-outs**: Embed 6 cuotas sin interes or 12 cuotas sin interes call-to-action in descripcion and foto 3 when ticket is above the marketplace threshold (500 MXN / 200 BRL / 25000 CLP / 200000 COP)
- **LATAM seasonal banners**: Hot Sale (MX May), Buen Fin (MX Nov), Black Friday (LATAM Nov), Navidad (Dec), Dia de las Madres (MX May 10), Carnaval (BR Feb-Mar), Vuelta a Clases (Feb-Mar CL CO). Banners are added to foto slot 1 and a banner line in descripcion

## Workflow

### Step 1: Listing Audit & Module Selection

Pull current listing data. Decide which modules to deploy: descripcion larga, ficha tecnica upgrade, foto overhaul, video, pack module, seasonal banner. Focus on the one with the largest conversion lift per effort.

### Step 2: Ficha Tecnica Completion

Fill every required attribute for the category. Use mercado-backend-keywords output to fill optional attributes with search-positive tokens. Verify against Mercado Libre catalog schema for the category to avoid rejection on publish.

### Step 3: Foto Storyboard

Produce or commission the 10-foto sequence. Each foto must be 1200x900 px minimum, white background for foto 1, lifestyle 2-3, scale and detail 4-6, comparison and certifications 7-9, badge 10. Localize overlays in Spanish or Portuguese per marketplace.

### Step 4: Video Script

Write the 30-60 second script. Open with a 3-second hook, demo the product, voice 3 beneficio lines, call out 6 cuotas sin interes if applicable, close with CTA. Render in 1080p with Spanish or Portuguese voiceover. Upload as MercadoLibre HD video.

### Step 5: Descripcion Larga

Write the 5000-char descripcion in structured sections. Lead with the hook and the 6 / 12 cuotas sin interes badge. Add bullet benefits, full especificaciones table, uso recomendado, garantia, FAQ block, and a Mercado Envios FREE or Full call-out if applicable.

### Step 6: Seasonal Overlay

For Hot Sale, Buen Fin, Black Friday, Navidad, Dia de las Madres, Carnaval, Vuelta a Clases overlay the seasonal banner on foto 1 and inject a banner line at the top of descripcion 14 days before the event and remove 7 days after.

### Step 7: Publish & Measure

Publish via seller.mercadolibre.com. Wait 7 days for MercadoLibre to re-index. Measure conversion rate, preguntas listing questions, add-to-cart ratio. Iterate on the weakest module.

## Quick Mode

Provide the marketplace (MLM / MLB / MLC / MCO), category, and current listing URL. Output a descripcion larga draft + ficha tecnica gap list + 10-foto storyboard in under 90 seconds.


## Compliance Checklist

- **Titulo 60-char limit**: Keep titulo at or below 60 characters to avoid MercadoLibre truncation on desktop and mobile
- **Foto resolution**: All fotos must be 1200x900 px minimum, JPG or PNG, white background for foto 1
- **Video format**: MP4 H.264, max 200 MB, max 60 seconds, HD 1080p preferred
- **Descripcion 5000-char limit**: Stay below 5000 chars including spaces and HTML
- **No external links in descripcion**: MercadoLibre strips http / https links from descripcion. Use preguntas frecuentes for cross-reference
- **Ficha tecnica category match**: Wrong category schema triggers catalog rejection on publish
- **Copyright-safe media**: Use original fotos only. Stock fotos flagged by MercadoLibre IP-protection get pulled
