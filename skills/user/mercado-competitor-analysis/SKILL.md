# Mercado Libre Competitor Analysis 🌎🌎

Deep-dive competitor intelligence across Mercado Libre marketplaces. Pinpoint exactly why the top 5 sellers win the buy box on each keyword and what gap you can close.

## When to Use

Activate this skill whenever the user mentions:
- 美客多竞品分析, 美客多对手分析, 美客多调研, 美客多竞品对比
- Mercado Libre competitor analysis, 美客多竞品拆解
- Analisis de competencia mercado libre, analisis de la competencia
- Analise de concorrencia mercado livre, concorrentes MLB
- MercadoLider benchmark, who wins the buy box
- Top 5 sellers for keyword X on MLM / MLB / MLC / MCO
- Compare my listing vs competitor

## Capabilities

- **Listing teardown**: Fetch and dissect competitor titulo (60 chars), ficha tecnica fields, descripcion (5000 chars), 10 fotos, video, and variante structure to extract their keyword footprint and ranking drivers
- **Reputation & trust audit**: Score competitors on MercadoLider tier (bronce / plata / oro), ventas concretadas last 60 / 90 days, calificaciones 4.x star distribution, % comentarios negativos, and reputation trend
- **Fulfillment moat mapping**: Compare Mercado Envios gratis, Full (mercado envios warehouse), Flex (same-day), and standard categories. Full sellers win 25-40 percent more buy box on hot keywords
- **Mercado Ads spend estimation**: Reverse-engineer competitor PADS share of voice, brand ads presence (MLB only), display retargeting footprint, and cuota investment level
- **Pricing & cuotas strategy**: Map competitor price ladder, 6 cuotas sin interes adoption, 12 cuotas sin interes on tickets above 500 MXN / 200 BRL, and discount-event timing (Hot Sale, Buen Fin, Black Friday)
- **Seasonal event readiness**: Check whether competitors ran Hot Sale / Buen Fin / Black Friday deals last cycle and how their listing was adapted (banner, deal price, cuotas count)
- **Multi-marketplace benchmark**: Same competitor ASIN-style MLMID across MLM, MLB, MLC, MCO with normalized currency and tax-ID friction note

## Workflow

### Step 1: Define the Battlefield

Identify the keyword, marketplace (MLM / MLB / MLC / MCO / MLA), and target buyer intent (research vs ready-to-buy). Confirm the category path so ficha tecnica field expectations are correct.

### Step 2: Enumerate Top 10 Competitors

Pull the organic top 10 for the keyword on the target marketplace. Note each MLMID, seller nickname, location, MercadoLider badge, ventas concretadas last 60 days, and full vs Flex vs standard flag. Filter to top 5 by 60-day ventas.

### Step 3: Listing Teardown

For each top 5 competitor extract:
- Titulo full text and character count
- Ficha tecnica completeness (which attributes are filled vs empty)
- Descripcion keyword density and benefit-led structure
- Foto count, white-background ratio, lifestyle shot count
- Video presence and length
- Variantes count and pricing ladder across sizes / colors

### Step 4: Reputation & Fulfillment Audit

Score each competitor on:
- MercadoLider tier and time held
- Calificaciones 4-5 star share, neutral / negative share with root-cause tags
- 60 / 90 day ventas trend (rising vs flat vs declining)
- Fulfillment: Mercado Envios gratis / Full / Flex / cross-border

### Step 5: Mercado Ads & Cuotas Reverse-Engineering

Infer Mercado Ads presence from listing position stability, brand ads appearance (MLB only), and rich content quality. Note whether competitor uses 6 cuotas sin interes or 12 cuotas sin interes on tickets above the market threshold. Check if competitor ran a deal badge during last Hot Sale / Buen Fin / Black Friday.

### Step 6: Weakness Matrix & Counter-Play

Build a 5-row weakness matrix: each competitor x dimension (price, ficha, fotos, video, full, cuotas, reputation). Highlight the cleanest gap and the easiest counter-play within 30 / 60 / 90 days.

## Quick Mode

Provide a single competitor MLMID or URL and the marketplace. Output a one-page teardown covering listing, reputation, fulfillment, ads, cuotas, and the top 3 counter-plays in under 90 seconds.


## Common Pitfalls

- **Titulo truncation**: MercadoLibre cuts the titulo in mobile search after ~38 characters. Front-load the highest-volume keyword in the first 35 chars
- **Foto 1 white background**: Non-white foto 1 loses 15-25 percent click-through vs MercadoLibre-catalog compliant white BG
- **Missing MercadoLider badge**: Sellers without MercadoLider bronze win 30-40 percent less buy box on competitive keywords regardless of price
- **No Full fulfillment**: Full sellers get the Mercado Envios FREE green badge which lifts CTR 10-18 percent on MLM / MLB
- **Cuota blind spot**: Not advertising 6 / 12 cuotas sin interes in descripcion leaves 20-40 percent conversion on the table for tickets above 500 MXN / 200 BRL
