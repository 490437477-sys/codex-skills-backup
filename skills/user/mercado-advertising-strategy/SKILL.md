# Mercado Libre Advertising Strategy 🌎🌎

Design a full-funnel Mercado Ads strategy that pairs PADS, Display, and Brand ads to your MercadoLider tier, Full status, and LATAM event calendar.

## Capabilities

- **Funnel architecture**: Map top-funnel Brand ads (MLB) and Display prospecting to mid-funnel PADS categoria to bottom-funnel PADS long-tail and Display retargeting for cart-abandoners and product-viewers
- **Budget allocation**: Per-category budget split for MLM / MLB / MLC / MCO in MXN BRL CLP COP with currency normalization to USD for comparison. Default split 60 percent PADS / 30 percent Display / 10 percent Brand ads where available
- **Bid calculation**: Suggest max-CPC bids per keyword using target ACoS 15-25 percent, average order value, and category commission 11-17 percent. Adjust for MercadoLider tier (oro gets 10-15 percent rank boost) and Full status (eligible boost on free-shipping keywords)
- **Spanish + Portuguese keyword structure**: Build campaign ad groups by intent (brand, category generic, long-tail, competitor). Include singular + plural + accent + synonym coverage (audifono, audifonos, auriculares, cascos / fone, fones, headphone, headset)
- **Seasonal ramp-up**: Pre-event budget lifts for Hot Sale Mexico (mid-May), Buen Fin (mid-Nov), Black Friday LATAM (late Nov), Navidad (Dec), Dia de las Madres (MX May 10), Carnaval Brazil (Feb-Mar), Vuelta a Clases CL/CO (Feb-Mar). Default 3x budget 7 days pre-event
- **Cuota bidding tactic**: On tickets above 500 MXN / 200 BRL / 25000 CLP / 200000 COP bid up on 6 cuotas sin interes and 12 cuotas sin interes keywords which convert 20-40 percent higher in MX/BR
- **ACoS optimization loop**: Weekly search-term review, 14-day negative keyword refresh, bid down on ACoS over 30 percent, bid up on ACoS under 12 percent with strong volume

## Workflow

### Step 1: Goal & Marketplace Definition

Set the goal: launch, scale, defend, or seasonal push. Pick the marketplace. Confirm the seller has the right tax-ID (RFC MX, CNPJ BR, CUIT AR, RUT CL) and MercadoLider tier if any. Note Full status.

### Step 2: Funnel Architecture

Design the campaign stack:
- Brand ads: top-funnel awareness, MLB only
- Display prospecting: cold audience lookalikes
- PADS categoria: mid-funnel category keywords
- PADS long-tail: bottom-funnel high-intent
- Display retargeting: cart-abandoners, product-viewers, past buyers

### Step 3: Budget Allocation

Allocate monthly budget across the stack. Default 60 / 30 / 10. Adjust for tier: MercadoLider bronce can lean 70 percent PADS. MercadoLider oro should re-balance to 50 PADS / 30 Display / 20 Brand to defend market share.

### Step 4: Keyword & Ad Group Build

For each campaign build ad groups with Spanish + Portuguese keyword sets:
- Brand ad group: brand + brand aliases (defensive)
- Category ad group: generic category terms
- Long-tail ad group: 3+ word phrases with high intent
- Competitor ad group: top-3 competitor brand names (where policy permits)

Add market-specific synonyms per country.

### Step 5: Bid & Cuota Setup

Set max-CPC per keyword using ACoS target formula. Flag 6 / 12 cuotas sin interes eligible tickets and increase bid 15-25 percent on those keyword groups to win on installment-intent traffic.

### Step 6: Seasonal Ramp-Up

Build a 90-day event calendar overlay. For Hot Sale, Buen Fin, Black Friday, Navidad, Dia de las Madres, Carnaval, Vuelta a Clases schedule the budget lift, the creative refresh, and the post-event cooldown.

### Step 7: Measure & Iterate

Weekly review of ACoS, ROAS, ventas from ads, share of voice. Bi-weekly negative keyword refresh. Monthly tier-up review: bid to climb from MercadoLider bronce to plata to oro using consistent ROAS.
