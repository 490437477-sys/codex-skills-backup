# noon's Hijri Calendar x PPC Action Sheet

> noon's audience converts differently across the Hijari year. Most noon Ads spikes happen around **Ramadan** (discovery + gifting surge), **Eid al-Fitr** (gift-giving peak), **Hajj season** (pre-travel premium for tech), **back-to-school** (Aug-Sep Gregorian), and **White Friday** (Black Friday analog, late November). Use this sheet to set **campaign launch dates, budget pacing, and bid-boost windows** 4-6 weeks in advance.

## How to Read This Table

Each row gives:

- **Hijri date** (anchor) + approximate Gregorian window
- **Phase** (pre-season / peak / post-season)
- **PPC action** (budget, bid, campaign)
- **Keyword add-ons** to push
- **Watch-out** (CPC inflation risk, inventory risks)

> All dates are approximate (Hijri calendar depends on moon sighting). Confirm via Saudi Supreme Court announcement 1-2 days before the event. Plan campaigns using the **conservative start** date and budget for an extra 3-5 days of overlap to capture late starters.

## Seasonal Campaign Playbook (STEM / Robotics kit SKUs)

### 1. Ramadan Window (4-6 weeks out from 1 Ram)

**Dates 2026:** Feb 18 - Mar 19 | **Phase:** Discovery + pre-Eid gifting build-up

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** Increase to 1.5x normal daily budget
- **Bid:** Add +20% to top-performing Manual campaigns (Auto already inflates CPC naturally)
- **New campaign:** "Ramadan AR Gift" - exact-match Arabic gift basket keywords
- **Dayparting:** Increase bids 21:00-02:00 (iftar-night browsing) by +30%
- **Negative keywords:** Add greeting-only terms (text triggers, not buying intent)

**Keyword Add-Ons (Arabic):**
- Ramadan gift
- Eid gift for kids
- Educational gift
- STEM gift
- Ramadan project

**Watch-out:** CPC inflation 40-60% in week before Eid. Don't overbid on generic terms - focus on gift-intent terms.

### 2. Eid al-Fitr Window (1-2 weeks after Ramadan ends)

**Dates 2026:** Mar 20 - Apr 5 | **Phase:** Peak gifting - biggest single conversion spike

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** 2x normal daily budget (highest single window)
- **Bid:** Top 20% increase on best-converting keywords
- **New campaign:** "Eid Gift" - gift-intent keywords, separate budget pool
- **Promo:** Bundle pricing ("Eid gift pack: arm + expansion kit")
- **Inventory:** Confirm FBN stock 14 days before (1 Dhul Hijjah is also late, so two Eids back-to-back)

**Keyword Add-Ons (Arabic):**
- Eid gift ideas
- Kids gift
- Programmer gift
- Educational toy for adults
- Boys gift

**Watch-out:** Inventory sell-out risk. Pre-position FBN stock 21-30 days before Eid.

### 3. Hajj Season (3 weeks before 1 Dhul Hijjah to Eid al-Adha)

**Dates 2026:** May 5 - Jun 4 | **Phase:** Premium tech / travel gifting

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** Normal (1.0x) - Hajj audience is small for STEM kits
- **Bid:** No change
- **Niche campaign:** "Hajj Premium Tech" - focus on high-end Arduino / Raspberry Pi kits
- **Bundling:** Pair with travel accessories for Hajj gift packs

**Watch-out:** Low relevance for education kits. Skip if you don't have premium pricing.

### 4. Eid al-Adha (1 week after Hajj)

**Dates 2026:** Jun 4 - Jun 11 | **Phase:** Second gifting peak

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** 1.3x normal
- **Bid:** +15% on top 10% keywords
- **New campaign:** "Eid Adha Gift" - focus on family / father / son gifting
- **Promo:** "Father-son build" angle - quality time + learning

**Keyword Add-Ons (Arabic):**
- Father gift
- Father-son gift
- Family time
- Family activity

**Watch-out:** Lower volume than Eid al-Fitr. Don't over-invest if previous Eid didn't convert.

### 5. Back-to-School (Aug 15 - Sep 15 Gregorian)

**Dates 2026:** Aug 15 - Sep 15 | **Phase:** **HIGHEST STEM KIT SURGE**

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** 1.8x normal
- **Bid:** +25% on STEM / educational / for-kids keywords
- **New campaign:** "Back-to-School STEM" - exact-match STEM-intent keywords
- **Promo:** "School project kit" positioning
- **B2B hook:** Switch to Institutional campaign mode (Mode C in main SKILL.md) - target school procurement

**Keyword Add-Ons (Arabic):**
- School project
- Educational toys for schools
- Back-to-school gift
- Robot kit for school
- STEM kit in Arabic
- Arduino board for schools

**Watch-out:** Schools buy in **bulk** (10-50 units). Have a B2B price tier or contact form ready.

### 6. KSA National Day (Sep 23) / UAE National Day (Dec 2)

**Dates 2026:** Sep 23 / Dec 2 | **Phase:** Patriotism-driven gifting

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** 1.4x normal (1-2 weeks pre-event)
- **Bid:** +20% on gift-intent keywords
- **New campaign:** "National Day" - Arabic-only, brand-aligned
- **Promo:** "Proudly built in KSA" or "Made for the Kingdom" - taps patriotism

**Keyword Add-Ons (Arabic):**
- National day gift
- National gift
- Gift for sons of the nation
- Saudi product [KSA only]

**Watch-out:** Conversion intent varies - patriots buy, not all browsers. Focus spend 3-5 days around the event, not full month.

### 7. White Friday (Last Friday of November)

**Dates 2026:** Nov 27 | **Phase:** **PEAK SALES - noon Black Friday**

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** 3x normal (highest single budget day)
- **Bid:** +30% across all campaigns during the 4-day event window
- **New campaign:** "White Friday" - discount-led, aggressive bid
- **Promo:** Deep discount (15-30% off list price)
- **Inventory:** Pre-position FBN stock 30 days before - sell-out risk is real

**Keyword Add-Ons (Arabic + English):**
- White Friday deals
- Discounts
- white friday deals
- noon white friday

**Watch-out:** CPC inflation 80-150% during event week. Don't bid on terms you can't convert profitably at 30% ACoS.

### 8. Islamic New Year (1 Muharram)

**Dates 2026:** Jun 26 | **Phase:** Low commercial - skip or minimal

**PPC Action:**
- **Budget:** 0.8x normal (slight decrease - low intent)
- **Bid:** No change
- **No new campaign**

**Watch-out:** Hijri-themed keywords may spike search volume but conversion is low (reflection, not buying).

## Pre-Season Preparation Checklist (4-6 weeks out)

- [ ] **30+ days out:** FBN inventory check (target 60+ days of stock)
- [ ] **21 days out:** Launch new seasonal campaign with low budget (test)
- [ ] **14 days out:** Confirm search term data - pivot keyword list based on early signals
- [ ] **7 days out:** Scale budget 1.5-2x, push bid +20-30% on winners
- [ ] **3 days out:** Dayparting adjustments (iftar hours, late night)
- [ ] **Day-of:** Final inventory check, ensure auto-bid + budget caps off
- [ ] **+3 days post:** Begin wind-down budget, harvest converting search terms into permanent Manual campaigns
- [ ] **+7 days post:** Kill seasonal campaign, document learnings in next quarter's strategy
