## 4. Logistics Models Cross-Country

- Mercado Envíos — platform-fulfilled; integrates carrier network by country
- Full — Mercado Libre warehouse; cross-country available (MLB→MLM most active)
- Flex — same-city delivery operated by seller under platform SLA
- Cross Docking — bulk inbound to Mercado hub, then platform handles last mile
- Cross-border fulfillment — Full origin country to destination country

## 5. Localization Quick Reference

- Argentina — castilian Spanish; units metric; sizing matters for fashion listings
- Brazil — Portuguese only; portuguese titles are heavily weighted in search
- Mexico — es-MX; coexists with US buyer expectations on sizing and English terms
- Chile — es-CL; closer to Mexican Spanish than Argentine
- Colombia — es-CO; note dialect terms (e.g., computador vs computadora)

## 6. Operational Caveats

- Currency controls: Argentina ARS may require local collection account; settlement delays common
- Brazilian NF-e: every shipped order needs a fiscal document (NF-e 4.00 or newer)
- Mexican CFDI: every invoice must be CFDI 4.0 with complemento de pago when applicable
- Tax ID format mismatches are the top cause of account suspension — validate before listing
- Holidays differ by country — see reference calendario-latam.md for full list

## 7. Decision Heuristic

- Start with MLM if your home country is outside LatAm and shipping from Asia/US: best customs flow + IVA recovery via certification
- Start with MLB if you can issue NF-e and accept longer payment terms
- Use MLA only with local entity or bonded 3PL (FX access otherwise blocked)
- MLC and MCO are good test markets for premium / SKU-light categories

## 11. Common Cross-Marketplace Mistakes

- Treating MLM and MLC as identical — different IVA, different bank MSI programs
- Listing the same Spanish content on MLA and MLM without localization; es-AR vs es-MX vocab differs
- Skipping Brazilian Portuguese translation on MLB — search relevance drops dramatically
- Underestimating customs cost for cross-border into Argentina due to FX access friction
- Forgetting that RUT format in Chile and RUT format in Uruguay differ; do not copy
