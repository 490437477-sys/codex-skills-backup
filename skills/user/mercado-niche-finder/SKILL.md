# Mercado Libre Niche Finder 🌎🌎

Discover profitable niches across LATAM marketplaces with high demand and manageable competition. Native Spanish + Portuguese + Mercado Envios + Full + MercadoLider context.

## When to Use

Activate this skill whenever the user mentions:
- 美客多蓝海, 拉美选品, 拉美蓝海, 美客多小众类目, 美客多细分类目

- nicho mercado libre, nicho mercado livre, mercado libre que vender
- o que vender no mercado livre, nicho lucrativo LATAM
- Blue ocean MLM, underserved category MLB, white-space MercadoLider
- Find category with low Full coverage + high MELI Trends growth
- Seasonal niche: Hot Sale Mexico, Buen Fin, Black Friday, Navidad, Dia de las Madres

## Capabilities

- **MELI Trends gap detection**: Cross-reference official tendencias.mercadolibre.com.{mx,br,cl,ar,co} weekly trending searches against existing listing density to surface rising-demand low-supply pockets
- **Full fulfillment coverage gap**: Map categories where demand exists but Full (mercado envios warehouse) coverage is below 30 percent, signaling logistics moat opportunities
- **MercadoLider white-space**: Detect sub-categories dominated by low-reputation green sellers where a MercadoLider badge wins the buy box within 60-90 days
- **Cuotas-friendly price band**: Filter niches by average ticket that supports 6 or 12 cuotas sin interes (interest-free installments) on Mercado Pago, which lifts conversion 20-40 percent in MX/BR
- **LATAM seasonality overlay**: Score Hot Sale (MX May), Buen Fin (MX Nov), Black Friday, Navidad, Dia de las Madres (MX May), Carnaval (BR Feb-Mar), and Back to School (Feb-Mar CL/CO)
- **Cross-market opportunity ranking**: One niche scored across MLM / MLB / MLC / MCO simultaneously with currency normalization MXN BRL CLP COP and tax-ID friction callout (RFC MX, CNPJ BR, CUIT AR)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Seed & Category Mapping

Pick a starting category or seed keyword. Use tendencias.mercadolibre.com.mx or .com.br plus Google Trends geo=MX BR CL CO to identify the macro bucket. Cross-reference with the official Mercado Libre catalog taxonomy to land on the right sub-category.

### Step 2: Demand Signal Triangulation

For each candidate niche collect three signals:
1. MELI Trends 30-day rising terms (top 20 percent growth)
2. Autocomplete depth using a-z suffix expansion in Spanish and Portuguese
3. Google Trends LATAM 12-month curve with geo filter

A niche passes if at least two of three signals show positive slope and 1000+ weekly searches on the target marketplace.

### Step 3: Supply & Competition Density

Count listings per niche per marketplace, segment by seller reputation (MercadoLider oro/plata/bronce vs verde), segment by fulfillment (Full vs Flex vs Mercado Envios standard). A blue-ocean niche typically has 200-2000 listings, less than 15 percent MercadoLider penetration, and less than 25 percent Full coverage.

### Step 4: Profit & Friction Filter

Compute landed cost per unit, apply commission 11-17 percent per category, Full storage fee, and Mercado Envios shipping subsidy if any. Discard niches where contribution margin drops below 18 percent. Note tax-ID friction: Mexico requires RFC, Brazil requires CNPJ, Argentina requires CUIT, Chile requires RUT for invoicing.

### Step 5: Seasonality & Event Overlay

Layer the LATAM event calendar. Hot Sale Mexico mid-May drives 5-8x category lift in electronics, beauty, fashion. Buen Fin mid-November drives 4-6x lift. Dia de las Madres May 10. Navidad Dec 1-24. Carnaval Brazil Feb-March lifts travel, beauty, fashion.

### Step 6: Score & Output

Score 1-10 across demand, competition, margin, seasonality, full-coverage moat. Output top 5 niches with a 90-day entry plan.
