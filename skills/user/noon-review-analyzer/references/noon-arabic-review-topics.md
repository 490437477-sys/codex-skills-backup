# noon Arabic Review Topic Clusters

Used by: noon-review-analyzer, noon-listing-optimization, noon-product-research.

MENA buyers write reviews in distinctive patterns. This reference maps the most common Arabic review topics to specific phrases, so the reviewer-analyzer can cluster complaints reliably and surface MENA-specific feature requests.

## MENA-specific feature requests

These are requests that frequently appear in MENA reviews and signal unmet market demand:

### Electrical

- Voltage 220V native (no adapter needed)
- UK 3-pin plug (for KSA/UAE) OR EU 2-pin plug with adapter
- Arabic on device menu / manual
- Right-hand drive car charger variants

### Religious / cultural

- Halal certification (food, cosmetics, supplements)
- Qibla finder (for watches, apps)
- Prayer time alerts
- Quran app pre-installed (for tablets)
- Hijri calendar support

### Family

- Larger size variants (MENA households average 6+ people)
- Multi-pack options (3-pack socks, 6-pack underwear)
- Bilingual packaging (Arabic + English)
- Modest / covered design options

### Climate

- Cooling / ventilation features (for hot climate)
- UV protection (sunglasses, fabrics)
- Dust-resistant (electronics)
- Sweat-resistant (makeup, watches)
- Heat-resistant food containers

## Recommended review response templates

### Response to size complaint (apparel)

```
شكراً لك على تقييمك. نأسف لعدم مطابقة المقاس لتوقعاتك.
نوصي بمراجعة جدول المقاسات في صفحة المنتج قبل الطلب.
يسعدنا مساعدتك في إيجاد المقاس المناسب.
[Translated: Thank you for your review. We apologize the size did not match your expectations. We recommend reviewing the size chart on the product page before ordering. We are happy to help you find the right size.]
```

### Response to shipping delay

```
نشكرك على تقييمك ونعتذر عن التأخير في التوصيل.
[Details about the cause]
نعمل على تحسين أوقات التوصيل لجميع طلبات FBN.
[Translated: Thank you for your review and we apologize for the shipping delay. We are working to improve delivery times for all FBN orders.]
```

### Response to quality complaint

```
نشكرك على ملاحظتك. نأخذ جودة المنتج على محمل الجد.
يرجى التواصل مع خدمة العملاء لاستبدال أو استرداد.
سنعمل مع المورد لتحسين [specific issue].
[Translated: Thank you for your feedback. We take product quality seriously. Please contact customer service for replacement or refund. We will work with the supplier to improve [specific issue].]
```
